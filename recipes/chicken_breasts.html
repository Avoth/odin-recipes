Chicken Breasts

Make this top-rated recipe with just five ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Creole Seasoning
  • Chicken Broth

Steps

  1. Season Chicken - Coat the chicken breasts with olive oil, then sprinkle both sides with salt and Creole seasoning.
  2. Bake Chicken - Place the seasoned chicken in a broiler pan
  3. Finish With Pan Sauce - Pour the chicken broth into the pan you used to bake the chicken
