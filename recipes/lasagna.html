Lasagna

Make our most popular lasagna today to find out what all the fuss is about!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Sugar
  • Spice
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta sauce
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest befor serving
